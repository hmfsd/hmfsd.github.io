About

10 years of House Music in San Diego.

House Music Fridays

Every Friday · 9 PM – 2 AM · Air Conditioned Lounge

Est. 2015 · San Diego, CA

For the last 10 years, House Music Fridays brings you exceptional talent every week in a venue where everyone's individuality is appreciated and encouraged. It is the only place for true House Music heads in San Diego to reside on Friday nights. Hosted weekly by veteran House DJ and producer Matthew Brian known for expertly mixing deep, jackin and soulful tunes. It's the place where the best up & coming local artists perform alongside legendary house DJs like Grammy award winner Stacy Kidd, DJ Heather, Jesse Saunders, Gene Hunt, Tony Powell, Demarkus Lewis, and many more.

Air Conditioned Lounge

4673 30th Street · San Diego, CA 92116

Located in the heart of North Park between Adams Ave & Madison Ave, the Air Conditioned Lounge is San Diego's home for House Music Fridays every week. A neighborhood bar with great sound, intimate vibes, and a dancefloor that brings people together.
